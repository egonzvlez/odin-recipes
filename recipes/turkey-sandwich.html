Turkey Sandwich

Why the turkey?

Its very simple and easy to make especially if you are short on time. You can make it for school lunches, road trips, and picnics.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Deli turkey
  • Provolone cheese

How to make a turkey sandwich

  1. Spread Mayonnaise on two slices of whole wheat bread. Then layer the turkey, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese on one slice. Top with the other slice of bread.
  2. Cut the sandwich in half.
  3. Enjoy!
