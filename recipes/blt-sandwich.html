The simple, but amazing BLT Sandwich

Why the BLT is amazing

The BLT is easy to make need only a couple Ingredients, but it still contains a powerful flavor profile. Honestly, its just easy and quick to make when time isn't your friend

Ingredients

  • Toasted Bread
  • Bacon
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salad Supreme Seasoning
  • Tomato Slices

How to make a BLT sandwich

  1. Cook bacon in a skillet or frying pan. Place on paper towel to drain grease.
  2. Toast 2 slices of whole wheat bread and spread each with mayonnaise on one side while still warm from toaster. On the top slice of bread, sprinkle salad supreme seasoning.
  3. On the bottom slice, layer bacon, tomato, cucumber slices, lettuce, and avocado.
  4. Top the sandwich, and enjoy while toast and bacon are still warm
